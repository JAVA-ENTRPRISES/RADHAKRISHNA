The 4-Step Process

01

Initial Inquiry

Fill out our digital enquiry form or visit our campus for a guided tour of our facilities.

02

Registration

Submit the formal application along with a nominal registration fee and academic transcripts.

03

Interaction

A friendly interaction for Primary levels or a basic aptitude assessment for Middle/Secondary levels.

04

Confirmation

On selection, complete the admission formalities and fee payment to secure your seat.

Documents Required

  • Previous School Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Last Academic Report Card
  • 6 Passport size photographs of the student
